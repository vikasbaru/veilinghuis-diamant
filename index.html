History

On September 5, 1945, Derksen organized its first auction, making Derksen Veilingbedrijf the oldest Auction Company in Gelderland and the only Auction Company in Arnhem.

National Monument Valburg

National monument “Valburg” was built as a tobacco warehouse in 1883. Most tobacco came from the Betuwe: Bemmel, Elst and Valburg. The planter and trader in tobacco Jan Hendrik de Hartog also hails from this place.
